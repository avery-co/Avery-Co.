About

Meet Avery.

I grew up in a small town in Vermont with a lot of time to be creative and not much else to do with it — which turned out to be exactly the right training. I completed a Bachelor's degree in Communication with a concentration in Media Production and Criticism at George Mason University, which gave me a critical framework for understanding not just how to make content, but why it works.

My work spans placemaking and brand content, event coverage, talking-head series, corporate video, and personal creative projects — I write, film, edit, and produce, bringing an end-to-end perspective to every one. Much of it has been rooted in place: I've spent years building the brand voice for a downtown Washington, DC nonprofit, driving significant audience growth, producing viral moments, and even contributing to a Guinness World Record.

These days I'm based in DC, splitting my time between live music, yoga, the outdoors, and my cats. I'm an outgoing person who genuinely loves people and their stories — and I think that shows in the work.
